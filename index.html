About droneSAR

droneSAR develops high resolution radar imaging technology for sensing crops from commercial drones and small aircrafts. Our radar sensors provide their own illumination and are independent of sunlight and are not sensitive to sun angle, clouds or air moisture. And unlike optical sensors, radar sensors can see below the canopy. Our cloud based software analytics platform processes the raw radar sensor data into calibrated and geo-coded images that can be used to provide measures of vegetation indices, biomass, soil moisture and leaf area index. Our mission is to provide our customers with robust actionable insights to improve their crop yields.
